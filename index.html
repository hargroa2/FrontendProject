What is the UFC?

The UFC, which stands for the Ultimate Fighting Championship, is an organization that conducts mixed martial art events in a cage where two people fight each other using a variety of martial arts techniques and styles.

A fight within the octagon cage can be won in one of three ways:

Knockout

A fighter is knocked out by the other person to the point where they could be unconscious or unable to continue the round

Decision

When both fighters make it to the end of the fight and are each evaluated by a panel of 3 judges

Submission

When a fighter is in a manipulated grappling position that could cause a limb to break, for their lungs to no longer receive air via a choke, or when the losing opponent taps out when held down by the other fighter for other reasons
